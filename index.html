About Me

Hello! My name is Jovany Cardenas Vargas. I come from a family of two younger brothers and two older half-brothers. My parents are from Mexico and work in the strawberry fields. I was born and raised in Santa Maria, CA, and developed an interest in Computer Science during high school, starting with a Web Design class that sparked my passion for coding.

I graduated from Pioneer Valley High School in 2022 with a 3.6 GPA and continued my education at Allan Hancock College, where I am pursuing an Associate's in Science for Computer Science. My coursework includes CS111, CS112, CS131, and I am currently taking CS161. I plan to transfer to a four-year university like CalPoly SLO to further my education.

Outside of my academic pursuits, I am involved in various extracurricular activities, including SkillsUSA, the Computer Science Club, the Science and Engineering Club, and support programs like EOPS, CAN-TRIO, and MESA. I have achieved significant success in SkillsUSA, winning gold at the state level for two years and silver at the national level. I am competing again this year and have already won gold at the state level.

In addition to being a full-time student, I work at the Career Center at Allan Hancock College as a student worker, assisting students with job searches, resume creation, typing tests, and meeting with career counselors. I also help organize events like Career Expo and Career Carnival, and support various programs within the Career Center, including SkillsUSA and College Corps.

Coding Projects

Below are some of the coding projects I've worked on for SkillsUSA:

  • Encryption Program (2022-23 Regionals): Created a program that encrypted numbers into other numbers and decrypted them back.
  • Depreciation Value Calculator (2022-23 State): Developed a GUI-based calculator to determine the depreciation value of an item.
  • Future Value Calculator (2022-23 State): Built a GUI-based program to calculate the future value of an item using an interest rate over a specified number of years.
  • Anagram Program (2022-23 Nationals): Created a GUI-based program that generated anagrams from a given name.
  • 15x15 Grid Game (2022-23 Nationals): Developed a GUI-based game similar to Minesweeper, featuring a 15x15 grid with hidden bombs and a health system to avoid the bombs.
  • Escape Velocity Calculator (2023-24 Regionals): Created a program to calculate the velocity needed to escape Earth's orbit using the radius and altitude.
  • Log File Creator (2023-24 Regionals): Developed a program to save provided information as a log file.
  • Crafting Inventory System (2023-24 Regionals): Built a crafting inventory system where recipes can be made from available items.
  • Range Interpreter (2023-24 State): Built a function to interpret a string of comma and/or hyphen-separated range descriptions into an ordered array of integers, handling various edge cases and invalid data.
  • File Search Tool (2023-24 State): Created a program to search for filenames within a directory structure based on user input, including features for constrained search depth, infinite loop prevention, and a user-friendly interface for viewing file contents.
